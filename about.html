About me

Who am I?

I'm a visual and UI/UX designer based in Berlin, Germany. I have a background in Business Economics and have worked as a stylist and creative director for the past two years, followed by a year of UI/UX design. My background in business and fashion equipped me with the analytical and conceptual tools to transition into UI/UX design. Some of my skills include conducting research, problem-solving, and coming up with creative solutions to high-level objectives. I'm proficient in Figma, Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe InDesign. As a life-long learner, I constantly learn new techniques and programs to add to my skill set.
